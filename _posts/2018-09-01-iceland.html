Last month I travelled to Iceland with Ryan Sheppeck, Dan Cook, and Daniel Casson for a three day action-packed landscape photography trip. Luckily, it had just snowed as we arrived so many of the spots that we visited didn't have as many variables to consider, which suited me as I got to focus more on composition and experimenting with contrast.
