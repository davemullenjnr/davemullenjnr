Alvarez Kings

Brand design and cover artwork for Alvarez Kings, a four-piece indie/pop band from Sheffield, UK.

Here’s a collection of cover designs for the band’s singles and EPs since 2012. Alvarez Kings released their debut album in 2017 with Warner Bros Records.

Branding, Graphic Design, Photography
